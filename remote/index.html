Join in on the fun from anywhere in the world!

If you can't make it to MozFest, don't worry. There are lots of great ways to still get involved.

See what's going on

  • Chat along with the festival on Twitter using the #MozFest hashtag.
  • See updates and real-time pictures on the Mozilla Facebook account and on Flickr, using the #MozFest hashtag.
  • Read what people are making and doing on our blog.
  • Watch live video from the keynote speeches and announcements, all broadcast from Air Mozilla's indoor blimp.

Participate and organize

  • Participate from afar by completing the Mozfest Remotee Challenge.
  • Remix this Thimble make to tell us why you believe in an open web.
  • Organize your own “mini Mozfest” with your local community using these helpful guides.

Celebrate

  • Share your experiences of making, learning and teaching the web on Twitter using the #MozFest hashtag.
  • After sharing and teaching with others, collect your Web Literacy Skill Sharer badge.
